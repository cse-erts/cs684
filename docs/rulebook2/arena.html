1. Arena Configuration

  • Arena consists of two parts - Traverse Area and Parking Area.
  • Parking area consists of Parking Zone where robot can park itself.
  • Both parts are connected through a node.
  • Dimension of the line is 1.5cm and dimension of node is 3cm x 3cm.

2. Preparing the dummy cars

  • Material required:

    • Thermocol

  • Preparing dummy cars:

    • Teams will prepare 6 dummy cars using thermocol.
    • Size of dummy car is 15cm x 15cm x 15cm.

3. Placing the dummy cars on Arena

  • Dummy cars can be placed:

    • On a line in traverse area.
    • In a parking zone in parking area.

  • When placed on a line, cars will act as an obstacles. Teams have to bypass the obstacle by using wall-hugging algorithm.

  • When placed in a parking zone, parking area will be blocked. Teams have to find sufficient space in the parking zone to park the robot.
